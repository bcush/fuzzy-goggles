# Quickstart Guide

Dwarf Fortress Stuff

## UI

+ Combination of key presses (i.e., `b-w-c` for Build, Workshop, and specific
  workshop) which are case sensitive

+ `Esc`, arrow keys, `+` and `-` and `Enter` are your main keys
